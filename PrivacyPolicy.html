Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are downloaded to clients' hard drives via their Web browsers. This allows our systems to recognize the browsers of our clients and store items in their Shopping Carts during visits.

Most browsers' Help section will be more useful in explaining how to prevent the browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify the user when a new cookie is received, or how to disable cookies entirely. Cookies, on the other hand, allow you to take full advantage of our site's top features, and our company's personal recommendation is that the client accepts them.
